CR2 Products we implement

Three powerful platforms. One experienced team.

We implement and customise the full CR2 product suite, tailored to your bank's specific workflows, branding, and regulatory requirements.

🏦
Digital Banking — BankWorld
CR2's flagship platform enabling banks to deliver seamless digital experiences across retail, business, and agency banking channels.
  • Retail Banking portal & mobile app
  • Business Banking self-service
  • Agency Banking for last-mile reach
  • Custom UI branding & theming
  • Core banking integration
  • Multi-language & multi-currency
👛
Digital Wallet
A comprehensive financial ecosystem enabling banks to offer wallet services without requiring a full bank account — driving financial inclusion.
  • Low-cost wallet account setup
  • Remote payment capabilities
  • P2P transfers & bill payments
  • Merchant QR integration
  • KYC & onboarding workflows
  • Regulatory compliance config
💳
Payment Hub
CR2's Payment Hub embeds banks into the payments value chain — covering card issuing, ATM driving, and merchant acceptance end to end.
  • Card issuing & lifecycle management
  • ATM driving & monitoring
  • Merchant acceptance integration
  • Switch & routing configuration
  • HSM & encryption setup
  • Scheme certification support
What we deliver

Our implementation & customisation services.

From initial scoping to post-go-live support, we cover every phase of a CR2 deployment.

01
Implementation & Deployment
Full installation and configuration of CR2 BankWorld across your infrastructure — cloud, on-premise, or hybrid — with environment setup, parameter configuration, and go-live planning.
02
Core Banking Integration
Connecting CR2 to your existing core banking system (Temenos, Finacle, Flexcube, or custom) via APIs, ISO 8583, or middleware — ensuring real-time transaction processing.
03
UI & Product Customisation
Tailoring the CR2 front-end to match your bank's branding, language requirements, and UX standards — including custom screens, workflows, and product configurations.
04
HSM & Security Configuration
Setting up Thales payShield HSM integration, DUKPT key management, PIN block encryption, and card personalisation workflows within the CR2 Payment Hub environment.
05
Testing & Certification
End-to-end functional, integration, and regression testing. We support scheme certification (Visa, Mastercard) and host-to-host connectivity testing with payment networks.
06
Training & Handover
Comprehensive training for your operations, IT, and business teams — covering system administration, parameter management, monitoring, and day-to-day operations.
07
Post Go-Live Support
SLA-backed managed support after go-live — incident management, patch application, performance monitoring, and ongoing enhancements as your business grows.
08
Upgrade & Migration
Seamless upgrades from older CR2 versions to the latest BankWorld release — with data migration, regression testing, and zero-downtime cutover planning.
How we work

Our implementation methodology.
